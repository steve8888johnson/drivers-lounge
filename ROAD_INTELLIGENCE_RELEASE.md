# Drivers Lounge — Integrated Road Intelligence Release

This release adds one combined dashboard for:

- Truck parking forecasts and driver reports
- Route-adjusted diesel and DEF comparisons
- Commercial-vehicle weather alerts
- Construction, crash and restriction events
- Truck-stop amenity comparison
- Truck wash, mobile repair and service listings
- Existing scale-house reporting

## Important data note
The included records are demonstration data. Replace them with licensed/provider feeds and live driver submissions before marketing the information as real-time or complete.
