# Drivers Lounge All-in-One Public Beta

This package combines the clean branded homepage with the navigation-first and road-intelligence releases.

Included modules:
- Clean branded homepage with interstate-shield truck logo and "Built by drivers for drivers"
- Truck-profile trip planner with hazmat and oversize controls
- Route alternatives and route confidence foundation
- Scale-house reports
- Truck parking
- Diesel and DEF comparisons
- Commercial weather and road events
- Truck stops, CAT scales, washes, repair and driver services
- Carrier and broker directories and reviews
- Driver jobs, resume/passport, documents and dashboard
- Pilot-car jobs and community
- Forums/community foundation
- Petitions, feedback, support and advertising pages
- Supabase migrations 003 and 004

Important: live routing and nationwide commercial datasets require licensed data providers. Demo records are labeled as beta data.
