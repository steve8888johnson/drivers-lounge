# Drivers Lounge Production Beta v6

## New modules
- Driver dashboard with route briefing, HOS summary, road alerts and AI copilot
- Demo load center with equipment filters, RPM display and saved loads
- Driver marketplace for trucks, trailers, equipment and businesses
- Expanded driver-services directory
- Upgraded carrier intelligence interface
- Upgraded community rooms and report workflow
- Global driver report modal
- Local saved items, applications, reports and AI responses

## Important limitations
- Load, carrier and marketplace records are demonstration data.
- Live freight requires verified shipper/carrier onboarding and payment/compliance systems.
- AI answers are deterministic beta guidance, not regulatory or legal advice.
- Navigation is still not certified commercial routing.
